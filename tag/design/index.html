Hi! I'm Niranjan. A Product Designer from New Delhi, India.

I have been working in the design industry for more than 6 years now and I have made peace with the fact that Design’s main job is to simplify things, make it live longer, and help make humans live a happier life. It is something which needs a balance of both creative and analytic side of my brain.

My work is heavily inspired by Dieter Ram’s and John Maeda’s principles around simplicity. I am a curious soul who wants to know who, how, what, and why behind everything around me.
