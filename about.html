Hello,

I am Niranjan Gupta. A Front End Engineer turned User Experience Designer from New Delhi, India.

I like building products which make the world a little less painful. Previously I worked with Elucidata, Paralleldots, Surgge( closed now ), bhane, and Tapzo. That said I am always up for solving the next problem and working with like-minded people.

As a UX Designer, my goal is to create user-friendly, scalable and innovative experiences which focus on understanding both customer and business needs. During my work, I try to define design goals, business goals and then selecting the right user-centered design methodologies to craft the best experience. I combine my UX knowledge with Graphic Design, Front End Engineering skills to create feasible interfaces which are user-friendly and aesthetically pleasing. When I am not working, I enjoy watching movies, fiddling with my guitar, reading blogs and experimenting with my cooking skills.

I have been working in the design industry for about 6 years now and I have made peace with the fact that Design’s main job is to simplify things, make things live longer, and help make humans live a happier life. It is something which needs a balance of both creative and analytic side of my brain. My works are heavily inspired by Dieter Ram’s and John Maeda’s principles around simplicity. I am a curious soul who wants to know who, how, what, and why behind everything around me.
